About — Employee Performance Prediction

The Employee Performance Prediction System uses Machine Learning and productivity analytics to predict employee performance levels and identify efficiency trends inside the organization.

The model evaluates key performance indicators such as:

  • Target productivity vs achieved output
  • Overtime & idle time behavior
  • Workload capacity & team efficiency
  • Time-based and seasonal performance trends

The system helps HR managers and supervisors make data-driven decisions for:

  • Performance monitoring & improvement
  • Workforce planning & scheduling
  • Preventing productivity degradation
  • Identifying under-performance risk early

Performance results are categorized as Low, Medium, or Highly Productive and visualized through interactive charts and dashboards.
